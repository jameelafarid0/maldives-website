Smart Budgeting for Students

Make the most of your Maldives adventure without breaking the bank. Discover practical money-saving tips, affordable travel hacks, and ways to enjoy paradise on a student budget

Budget-Friendly Travel Tips

Explore Free or Low-Cost Activities

Discover beaches, local markets, and public parks that require little or no money. The Maldives offers beautiful scenery that can be enjoyed without spending a fortune.

Use Local Transportation

Instead of expensive private transfers, opt for ferries and local buses. They’re reliable, safe, and give you an authentic local experience.

Take Advantage of Student Discounts

Always carry your student ID. Many tours, attractions, and even restaurants offer special rates for students if you ask!

Eat Like a Local

Skip the expensive resorts. Try budget-friendly cafes and local eateries for authentic Maldivian food at a fraction of the cost.

Stay in Budget Accommodations

Choose guesthouses or hostels instead of resorts. They’re cheaper, often more social, and still comfortable and safe for students.
