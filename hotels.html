Booking Hotels in the Maldives

Explore the best budget-friendly hotel options handpicked for international students. Whether you're looking for basic comfort or eco-conscious stays, we've got you covered.

Find the Right Stay

The Maldives offers a variety of affordable hotels tailored for international students. This guide helps you choose based on budget, comfort, and location, ensuring a relaxing stay without overspending.

Types of Hotels Available

Ideal for students on a tight budget. These no-frills hotels offer essential amenities like Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and clean rooms. Expect prices between $50–$100 per night.
